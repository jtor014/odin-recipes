Cheese Toastie

A cheese toastie - yum!

First you have to source the best cheese you can find. This isn't easy so I suggest you real do your research

Once you have don that this is the recipe for you!

Ingredients

  • Various delicious cheeses
  • Fresh white sourdough
  • Butter
  • More butter

Equipment

  • Stove
  • Frying pan
  • Spatula

Steps

  1. Put it together as best you can
  2. Come up with some way to apply heat
  3. Focus mainly on plating, cooking is secondary
